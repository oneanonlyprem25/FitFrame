How to Crop Images for Instagram & Social Media

FitFrame Pro is a secure, client-side image resizing tool designed for creators. Whether you need a 4:5 portrait for an Instagram Feed, a 9:16 story layout, or a square 1:1 profile picture, our tool ensures your images fit perfectly without losing quality.

Why use WebP format?

We support exporting to WebP, a modern image format that provides superior lossless and lossy compression for images on the web.

Privacy Assurance (Local Processing)

Unlike other online croppers, FitFrame processes your images locally in your browser. Your photos are never uploaded to a remote server.
